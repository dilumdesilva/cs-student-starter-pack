# Job Opportunities

A collection of resources for finding job opportunities in tech.

## Job Search Platforms

- [LinkedIn Jobs](https://www.linkedin.com/jobs/) - Professional networking and job search
- [Indeed](https://www.indeed.com/) - Job search engine
- [Glassdoor](https://www.glassdoor.com/) - Job listings with company reviews and salary data
- [AngelList](https://angel.co/) - Startup job board
- [We Work Remotely](https://weworkremotely.com/) - Remote job listings
- [Remote OK](https://remoteok.com/) - Remote job aggregator

## Tech-Specific Job Boards

- [Hacker News: Who's Hiring](https://news.ycombinator.com/ask) - Monthly job threads on Hacker News
- [Stack Overflow Jobs](https://stackoverflow.com/jobs) - Developer job listings
- [GitHub Jobs](https://github.com/about/careers) - Jobs at GitHub and open-source-friendly companies
- [Key Values](https://www.keyvalues.com/) - Find engineering teams that share your values

## Open Source Resources for Job Hunting

- [Remote Jobs](https://github.com/remoteintech/remote-jobs) - List of semi to fully remote-friendly companies in tech
- [Easy Application](https://github.com/j-delaney/easy-application) - Software engineering companies that have easy application processes
- [Awesome Remote Job](https://github.com/lukasz-madon/awesome-remote-job) - Curated list of remote working resources

## Interview Preparation

- [Tech Interview Handbook](https://github.com/yangshun/tech-interview-handbook) - Curated coding interview preparation materials
- [Coding Interview University](https://github.com/jwasham/coding-interview-university) - Complete study plan to become a software engineer
- [Awesome Interview Questions](https://github.com/DopplerHQ/awesome-interview-questions) - Curated list of interview questions across many technologies
